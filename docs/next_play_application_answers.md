## 1) Describe a time you used AI to solve a technical or workflow problem.

During my internship at Izzonet, one technical problem we had was answering common menstrual-health questions quickly inside the Android app without sending every request to a human expert or a heavy external service. Users often asked similar questions in different ways, and many included spelling mistakes, so a static FAQ page was not enough. I built a lightweight AI/NLP assistant inside the app using custom n-gram intent matching, Levenshtein distance for typo tolerance, and TF-IDF retrieval over a vetted knowledge base built from trusted sources. This let the app match questions more accurately and return answers much faster on-device. On our internal 300-question test set, it improved answer hit rate and reduced latency because responses were served locally with caching. It also made the workflow better for the team because we could keep updating the knowledge base and improve the experience without rebuilding the whole product logic.
